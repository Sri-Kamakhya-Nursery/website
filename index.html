About Us


Sri Kamakhya Nursery in kadiyam, East Godavari is not just a plant nursery… it’s a passion for greenery and a commitment to bring nature closer to every home. Started with a simple vision to create beautiful green spaces, we take pride in offering healthy, high-quality plants that thrive in our local climate. (" From flowering plants to fruit plants, indoor décor to avenue trees — every plant we grow is nurtured with care and dedication.") We believe that a single plant can transform a space, improve well-being, and spread happiness. That’s why we focus on providing not just plants, but complete guidance to help our customers grow their own green paradise. At Sri Kamakhya Nursery, we are growing more than plants… we are growing trust, beauty, and a greener future.

Founded by Pavan Kalyan Devagalla (S/o Satyanarayana)
